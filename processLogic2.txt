Based on the **Law on Motor Vehicles (Zakon o motornih vozilih - ZMV-1)** provided in the document, here is the end-to-end process for the lifecycle of a motor vehicle (from placing on the market to deregistration).

1. **Manufacturer** (User Task)
   - Submit application for vehicle type homologation or individual approval (Article 11, 16).
   - *Flow: Goes to Homologation Body.*

2. **Homologation Body** (Service Task)
   - Assess vehicle compliance with technical requirements and prescribed standards (Article 11, 12).
   - *Flow: Goes to Gateway (Compliance Check).*

3. **Homologation Body** (Gateway)
   - Is the vehicle type compliant with technical regulations?
   - *Flow (Yes): Goes to Issue Certificate.*
   - *Flow (No): Goes to Refuse Homologation.*

4. **Homologation Body** (Service Task)
   - Refuse homologation application due to non-compliance or danger (Article 12).
   - *Flow: Process End (for this vehicle instance).*

5. **Homologation Body** (Service Task)
   - Issue Homologation Certificate (Type Approval) or Individual Approval Certificate (Article 11, 16).
   - *Flow: Goes to Manufacturer.*

6. **Manufacturer** (User Task)
   - Issue Certificate of Conformity (COC) for every individual vehicle produced based on the approved type (Article 13).
   - *Flow: Goes to Owner.*

7. **Owner** (User Task)
   - Purchase vehicle and obtain insurance and proof of ownership (Article 25).
   - *Flow: Goes to Expert Organization.*

8. **Expert Organization** (Service Task)
   - Perform vehicle identification and assessment of technical status (if required for new/imported vehicles) (Article 20).
   - *Flow: Goes to Owner.*

9. **Owner** (User Task)
   - Submit application for vehicle registration at a Registration Organization or Administrative Unit (Article 30).
   - *Flow: Goes to Registration Organization.*

10. **Registration Organization** (Service Task)
    - Verify registration prerequisites: ownership, insurance, homologation/COC, and fees (Article 25, 30).
    - *Flow: Goes to Gateway (Registration Check).*

11. **Registration Organization** (Gateway)
    - Are all conditions for registration met?
    - *Flow (Yes): Goes to Issue Documents.*
    - *Flow (No): Goes to Reject Registration.*

12. **Registration Organization** (Service Task)
    - Reject registration application (Article 30).
    - *Flow: Process End (until issues resolved).*

13. **Registration Organization** (Service Task)
    - Register vehicle, issue Traffic Permit (Prometno dovoljenje), and issue License Plates (Article 32, 33).
    - *Flow: Goes to Owner.*

14. **Owner** (User Task)
    - Operate vehicle in road traffic (Article 25).
    - *Flow: Goes to Technical Inspection Body (Periodic).*

15. **Technical Inspection Body** (Service Task)
    - Perform regular Technical Inspection based on statutory intervals (e.g., 4 years after first reg, then every 2 years) (Article 48, 50).
    - *Flow: Goes to Gateway (Inspection Result).*

16. **Technical Inspection Body** (Gateway)
    - Is the vehicle technically flawless (brezhibno)?
    - *Flow (Yes): Goes to Confirm Inspection.*
    - *Flow (No): Goes to Issue Rejection.*

17. **Technical Inspection Body** (Service Task)
    - Issue rejection report and set deadline for repair (Article 50).
    - *Flow: Goes to Owner (Loop to Repair).*

18. **Technical Inspection Body** (Service Task)
    - Confirm technical perfection and extend validity in the Traffic Permit (Article 50).
    - *Flow: Goes to Owner.*

19. **Owner** (User Task)
    - (Optional) Perform modifications/tuning on the vehicle (Article 52).
    - *Flow: Goes to Expert Organization.*

20. **Expert Organization** (Service Task)
    - (Optional) Inspect vehicle modifications and record changes (Article 52).
    - *Flow: Goes to Registration Organization (Update Data).*

21. **Inspector / Police** (Service Task)
    - Perform roadside check or inspection of the vehicle (Article 73, 76).
    - *Flow: Goes to Gateway (Roadside Check Result).*

22. **Inspector / Police** (Gateway)
    - Is the vehicle defective or non-compliant on the road?
    - *Flow (Yes): Goes to Sanction.*
    - *Flow (No): Goes to Owner (Continue Usage).*

23. **Inspector / Police** (Service Task)
    - Seize license plates, exclude vehicle from traffic, or issue fine (Article 73, 74).
    - *Flow: Goes to Owner (Must fix vehicle).*

24. **Owner** (User Task)
    - Apply for vehicle de-registration (Odjava vozila) due to sale, theft, destruction, or end of life (Article 39).
    - *Flow: Goes to Registration Organization.*

25. **Registration Organization** (Service Task)
    - Record de-registration in the database and collect license plates (unless kept by owner) (Article 39, 40).
    - *Flow: Process End.*
