Based on the provided document "Upravljani zahtevki po storitvah in incidentih v podjetju Telekom Slovenije (COBIT DSS02)", here is the step-by-step process extraction adhering to BPMN 2.0 semantics.

1.  **Report Issue or Request** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Uporabnik (User)
    *   **Flow:** To *Validate and Record Ticket*.
    *   *Note:* User reports via Self-service portal, Phone (080 1234), or E-mail.

2.  **Validate and Record Ticket** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow:** To *Categorize and Prioritize*.
    *   *Note:* Operator checks completeness and records key data (Contact, Location, Description, Service).

3.  **Categorize and Prioritize** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow:** To *Is Major Incident?*
    *   *Note:* Assigns category and priority (P1-P4) based on urgency/impact matrix.

4.  **Is Major Incident?** (Exclusive Gateway)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow (Yes):** To *Major Incident Process* (Link Event/End Process - Activates special protocol).
    *   **Flow (No):** To *Check Knowledge Base*.
    *   *Note:* Decision Point 1. Criteria: Affects >50 users or critical service.

5.  **Check Knowledge Base** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow:** To *Attempt First Call Resolution*.
    *   *Note:* Search for Known Errors or standard solutions.

6.  **Attempt First Call Resolution (FCR)** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow:** To *Is Resolved at Level 1?*
    *   *Note:* Operator tries to solve immediately (reset password, restart service).

7.  **Is Resolved at Level 1?** (Exclusive Gateway)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow (Yes):** To *Close Incident*.
    *   **Flow (No):** To *Escalate to Level 2*.
    *   *Note:* Decision Point 2.

8.  **Escalate to Level 2** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater (Level 1)
    *   **Flow:** To *Accept and Review*.
    *   *Note:* Assignment to specific group (Network, System, App, Desktop). System sends notification.

9.  **Accept and Review** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2)
    *   **Flow:** To *Identify Root Cause*.
    *   *Note:* Specialist picks from queue, reviews notes, contacts user if needed.

10. **Identify Root Cause** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2/3)
    *   **Flow:** To *Is Recurring Problem?*
    *   *Note:* Advanced diagnosis via remote access/logs.

11. **Is Recurring Problem?** (Exclusive Gateway)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2/3)
    *   **Flow (Yes):** To *Create Problem Record* (Service Task - linked to DSS03).
    *   **Flow (No):** To *Can Specialist Resolve Alone?*
    *   *Note:* Decision Point 5.

12. **Can Specialist Resolve Alone?** (Exclusive Gateway)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2/3)
    *   **Flow (Yes):** To *Implement Solution*.
    *   **Flow (No):** To *Escalate to Level 3 / External Vendor* (User Task/Link Event).
    *   *Note:* Decision Point 3.

13. **Implement Solution** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2/3)
    *   **Flow:** To *Verify Solution*.
    *   *Note:* Config fix, restart, patch, hardware replacement. If change management needed, coordinate with DSS04.

14. **Verify Solution** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2/3)
    *   **Flow:** To *Is Solution Confirmed by User?*
    *   *Note:* Specialist checks status and contacts user for confirmation.

15. **Is Solution Confirmed by User?** (Exclusive Gateway)
    *   **Actor:** Uporabnik (User) / Specialist
    *   **Flow (Yes):** To *Document Solution and Update KB*.
    *   **Flow (No):** To *Identify Root Cause* (Loop back to diagnosis).
    *   *Note:* Decision Point 4.

16. **Document Solution and Update KB** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist (Level 2/3)
    *   **Flow:** To *Update CMDB*.
    *   *Note:* Create or update Knowledge Base article.

17. **Update CMDB** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Specialist / CMDB Administrator
    *   **Flow:** To *Close Incident*.
    *   *Note:* Performed only if CIs (Configuration Items) were changed (hardware/software/IP).

18. **Close Incident** (Service Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Operater / Specialist (via System)
    *   **Flow:** To *Send Satisfaction Survey*.
    *   *Note:* Status changed to "Closed". Recorded solution, time worked, root cause.

19. **Send Satisfaction Survey** (Service Task)
    *   **Actor:** System (Automated)
    *   **Flow:** To *Analyze Trends and Report*.
    *   *Note:* Sent automatically 1 hour after closure.

20. **Analyze Trends and Report** (User Task)
    *   **Actor:** Service Desk Manager / IT Manager
    *   **Flow:** End Event.
    *   *Note:* Weekly/Monthly review of KPIs, SLA compliance, and CSAT scores.
